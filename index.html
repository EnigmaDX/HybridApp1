Introduction To Ashesi University

Ashesi University College is a tertiary liberal arts educational institution that is focused creating ethical entrepreneurial leaders for Africa and beyond.

MAJORS

Ashesi offers 4 majors:
  • Computer Science
  • Management Information Systems
  • Business Administration
  • Engineering
    • Computer Engineering
    • Mechanical Engineering
    • Electrical Engineering
